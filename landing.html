How it works

Four steps from logging
to life-saving

A continuous care loop that catches what doctors can't see between visits.

1
Patient logs
Medications, vitals, and symptoms via app, voice note, or SMS — even offline
2
AI analyzes
Detects patterns, flags risk, generates a patient brief for the next consult
3
Care responds
Caregivers are alerted, follow-ups are suggested, SOS is escalated if needed
4
Doctor reviews
Gets a 3-minute AI-generated brief before saying hello — no chart hunting
